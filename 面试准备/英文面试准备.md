# 英文面试准备

## 使用说明

你的 CET-6 是 436 分（刚过425及格线），策略是：
- **不装**。稿子用简单句、基础词汇，跟你真实水平匹配
- **不回避**。被问到英语水平，坦诚说"读写没问题，口语需要练"
- **准备几个关键词**。保险和技术的核心词汇提前背熟，问到项目能蹦出关键词就赢了

面试官对实习生的英语期待不是"流利如母语"，而是"能基本交流 + 有提升意愿"。

# 一、英文自我介绍（约 40 秒，95 词）

> Hello, I'm Wang Qibo, a second-year CS student at Shanghai Normal University.
>
> I'm interested in insurtech. I've studied the insurance business process — product design, underwriting, policy service, and claims. To apply what I learned, I built an AI underwriting assistant using Dify. I analyzed the underwriting logic, designed the workflow, tested different cases, and wrote documentation.
>
> I passed CET-6. My reading and writing are solid. Speaking is something I'm still working on — and that's part of why I want to join the EMEA team, to improve in a real working environment.
>
> Thank you.

## 逐句拆解 & 发音提示

| 句子 | 要点 |
|------|------|
| Hello, I'm Wang Qibo, a second-year CS student at Shanghai Normal University. | 最简单开场。CS 读作 "C-S"，不是 "computer science" |
| I'm interested in **insurtech**. | insurtech = insurance + technology，保险科技的标准说法。读 in-sur-tek |
| I've studied the insurance business process — product design, **underwriting**, policy service, and claims. | underwriting（核保）是核心词，读 un-der-rai-ting。claims（理赔）读 kleimz |
| To apply what I learned, I built an AI underwriting assistant using **Dify**. | Dify 读 di-fai |
| I analyzed the underwriting logic, designed the **workflow**, tested different cases, and wrote **documentation**. | workflow（工作流）读 werk-flo。documentation（文档）读 do-kiu-men-tei-shn |
| I passed CET-6. My reading and writing are solid. | solid = 扎实的。坦诚但不自卑 |
| Speaking is something I'm still working on — and that's part of **why I want to join the EMEA team**, to improve in a real working environment. | 把弱点变成动机——"正因为想提升口语，所以想来EMEA团队" |

# 二、可能被追问的英文问题 & 简答

## Q1: Tell me more about your project.

> Sure. It's a tool that helps with insurance underwriting. You type in your age, job, and health condition — like "I'm 30, a delivery driver, with high blood pressure." The system then checks the underwriting rules, calculates the risk, and gives a recommendation — standard, extra fee, postpone, or decline.
>
> One problem I solved: at first, the system couldn't find the right rules. Because the user says "blood pressure a bit high" but the rule book says "hypertension level 1, extra 20%." These two don't match well in search. So I added a step to translate casual language into structured queries first. After that fix, the search worked much better.

**要点**：用最简单的话把项目说清楚。不需要技术细节，把那个"口语vs规则不匹配"的坑讲出来就很好。

## Q2: Why do you want to work at eBao?

> Two reasons. First, eBao focuses on insurance technology, which is the field I want to grow in. Second, the EMEA team works with international clients, so I can use English in real projects — that's very different from just studying for an exam. I think real work experience is the fastest way to improve.

## Q3: What do you know about the insurance business?

> I know the main process: product definition, underwriting, policy issue, policy service, and claims. I spent time learning how underwriting works — it's about matching rules to risk factors. Different jobs have different risk levels. Health conditions affect the premium. Age matters too. All these factors combine to give a final underwriting decision.

## Q4: How do you handle something you don't understand?

> I first try to figure it out myself — search online, read documents. If I'm still stuck, I ask. I'd rather ask early than stay confused for a week. In my project, when the search wasn't working, I tested each part one by one until I found the root cause.

## Q5: Is there anything you want to ask me?

**准备一个反问**（显示你思考过）：

> What does a typical day look like for an intern in the EMEA team?

或者：

> What's the biggest challenge for someone new to the insurance industry?

# 四、应对英语卡壳的救命句

如果面试官用英语提问，你听不太懂：

> "Sorry, could you say that again a bit slower?"

如果听懂了一部分但不完全：

> "Let me make sure I understand — you're asking about [你猜的关键词], right?"

如果一个词想不起来怎么说：

> "Sorry, I'm not sure of the exact word in English. What I mean is [换一种方式解释]."

如果完全不知道怎么说：

> "That's a good question. Let me think for a moment."（停顿3秒，组织一下）

**不要做的事**：不要沉默、不要直接说"我不会"、不要用中文回答。

# 五、诚实策略：被问到"你英语怎么样"时

如果面试官是中国人用中文问：

> "读写没问题，CET-6过了。口语日常交流可以，但商务场景还需要练——这也是我投EMEA团队的原因，在真实工作环境里提升最快。"

如果面试官是外国人用英文问 "How's your English?"：

> "My reading and writing are fine. I can understand most things if spoken clearly. Speaking — I'm still improving. But I'm not afraid to make mistakes, and I learn fast in an English environment."

**核心原则**：承认不足 + 表达意愿 + 给出例子（比如你能写英文文档，你的项目文档就有英文版）。

# 六、面试前练习方法

1. **对着镜子把自我介绍讲 10 遍**，直到不需要看稿
2. **录音自己听**，找出发音最别扭的词，单独练
3. **把 Q1（讲项目）讲 5 遍**，这是最可能被追问的
4. **准备 2 个保险专业词汇的英文解释**——至少 underwriting 和 claims 要能随口说出
5. **如果时间够**，把简历上每个 bullet point 口译成英文说一遍，不会的词查好背下来
